🎯 Think you can read the computer's mind? Guess the secret number and prove it!

A fun and visually stunning 3D browser-based number guessing game where a random number between 1 and 100 is generated, and your mission is to guess it with the fewest tries possible. Built originally in C and brought to life on the web with Three.js, glassmorphism UI, and playful animations.


--🕹️ How to Play

(1.) 🌐 Open index.html in your browser,
(2.) 🔢 Enter a number between 1 and 100 in the input box,
(3.) 🖱️ Click the Guess! button or press Enter,
(4.) ⬆️ If you see "Higher Number Please!" → your guess was too low, go higher!,
(5.) ⬇️ If you see "Lower Number Please!" → your guess was too high, go lower!,and
(6.) 🎉 When you guess correctly → Confetti explodes and you win! 🏅,

🔄 Click Play Again to start a new round
🏆 Try to beat your Best Score — fewer guesses = better!
